Zoetrope Graphics

Short Intro Animation

This is an overly long intro video of Zoetrope graphics. Notice the recursive screen on the monitor of the SGI Indy, showing the same scene in wireframe mode. Along with that, there are various scattered refrences of future 3D models and diagrams on the floor. Lastly of course is the two notable props aside the zoetrope. An SGI indy, which many of the movies and games the computer is resposible for are all inspirations of mine, and a bolex style camera, noteworthy for its use in early stop motion animation. This video was planned to have a nice soundtrack and mechanical foley to go with it. however I've yet to add such.

Animation - Jordan Cruz
